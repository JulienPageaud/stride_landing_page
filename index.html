Why Stride?

Stride helps people to access banking services using only their mobile phone. Financial institutions rely on Stride to make their eligibility assessments. Stride includes an e-wallet service and is accessible through any device.

No paper. People can apply for a loan using any kind of mobile phone. Smartphone users can download the mobile app and cellphone users can text 4012.

Fast process. If you are eligible for a loan, you will receive a response very quickly. If not, we can help you to become eligible.

Simple exchanges. Your e-wallet will be credited directly. We will clearly explain the terms for paying back your loan.

We help financial institutions to improve their service to clients

Benefit from a dedicated dashboard with comprehensive client activity monitoring, live updates and a portfolio overview.

How it works

1. Sign up using your mobile number
2. Complete your personal details
3. Click on apply for a loan
4. Complete your loan details
5. The bank will review your application and you will be notified
6. Congratulations! You have received your loan

Where is Stride

Stride participates to the financial inclusion in South Africa, giving unbanked individuals access to a loan. This social initiative permits banks to enhance their participation to the South African economy while helping people to develop their financial responsibilities. The default rate calculated by Stride is similar to what banks obtain using traditional scoring methods.
